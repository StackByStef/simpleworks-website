## FAQ

**Section tag:** FAQ

**Headline:**
Questions I hear a lot.

**How long does a project take?**
Most projects launch within about a week from strategy call to live site. If there's something complex — custom integrations, large content migrations — it might take a little longer, but you'll know the timeline upfront.

**Can you fix my existing site or do I have to start over?**
Depends on what you're working with. If the bones are solid, we can revamp it. If it's the thing holding you back, we'll recommend starting fresh. We'll figure that out together on the strategy call.

**Is SEO included?**
Yes. Every site ships with optimized metadata, structured data, fast load times, mobile responsiveness, and clean semantic HTML. It's built into the process — not charged as an extra.

**What about AI search — will my site show up in ChatGPT or Google AI?**
That's built in too. We structure your content so AI systems can find, understand, and reference your business — not just link to it. It's called GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), and it's part of every build.

**What does "conversion-focused" mean?**
It means the site is designed to get visitors to do something — fill out a form, book a call, sign up, buy. The layout, the copy, the button placement — it's all intentional, not decorative.

**Do you manage hosting after launch?**
Yes. SimplWorks provides managed hosting and infrastructure so you don't have to deal with servers, databases, or deployments. We handle it.

**What does a website cost?**
Every project is scoped individually based on what you need. We'll cover pricing on the strategy call once we understand your goals and what needs to be built.

**Do you only build websites?**
Websites are the starting point. SimplWorks is growing — we're adding more tools and services to help businesses run smarter. The website is just the front door.
