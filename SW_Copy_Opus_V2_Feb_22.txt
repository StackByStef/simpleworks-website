**How long does a project take?**
About a week from strategy call to live site. If there's something complex — custom integrations, large content migrations — it might take a little longer, but you'll know the timeline upfront.

**Is SEO included?**
Yes. Every site ships with optimized metadata, structured data, fast load times, mobile responsiveness, and clean semantic HTML. Built into the process — not charged as an extra.

**Do you also optimize for AI search?**
Yes. Every site includes GEO — Generative Engine Optimization — so your business can be found and cited by AI systems like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overview. Most web designers aren't doing this yet. We build it in from the start.

**What does "conversion-focused" mean?**
It means the site is designed to get visitors to do something — fill out a form, book a call, sign up, buy. The layout, the copy, the button placement — it's all intentional, not decorative.

**Can you fix my existing site or do I have to start over?**
Depends on what you're working with. If the bones are solid, we can revamp it. If it's the thing holding you back, we'll recommend starting fresh. We'll figure that out together on the strategy call.

**What does a website cost?**
Every project is scoped individually based on what you need. We'll cover pricing on the strategy call once we understand your goals and what needs to be built.

**Do you only build websites?**
Websites are the starting point. Every site we build is designed to grow with your business — lead capture, email systems, hosting, and more are all part of the package.
